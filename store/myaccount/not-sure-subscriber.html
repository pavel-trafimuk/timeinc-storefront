Not Sure How You Subscribed?

To confirm that you are an iTunes subscriber, follow these steps:

  1. On your iPad's home screen, tap "Settings".
  2. On the left navigation bar, tap "iTunes & App Store".
  3. Tap on your Apple ID.
  4. Tap “View Apple ID”. You will be prompted to enter your iTunes password.
  5. Tap “Manage” under Subscriptions.

If it says "Active" next to your magazine, you have subscribed via iTunes. If it says "Expired" next to your magazine, you have subscribed via iTunes but your subscription is no longer active.

If the magazine is not listed, you did not subscribe via iTunes. If you subscribed elsewhere, tap the button below to verify your subscription and create your digital account.
