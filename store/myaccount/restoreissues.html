I'm a print subscriber and recently updated my ENTERTAINMENT WEEKLY app. Why don't I see any of my digital editions in the Library?

You will need to sign in before you can see your available issues.
1
In the Library, tap "Sign In" in the top left corner.

2
Download your available issues and enjoy! If you had previously downloaded any issues, they will need to be downloaded again in the new version before you can view them. (Please note, this process may take some time.) Going forward, all new issues will be available only in the updated version of this app.

If you are having problems verifying your subscription, please call 1-866-772-8791.

How do I restore my existing digital-only subscription or previously purchased issues?

You will need to restore your purchases using your Apple ID.
1
In the Library, tap the settings icon  at the top right of the screen.

2
Tap "Restore All Purchases" and enter your Apple ID.

3
Download your available issues and enjoy! If you had previously downloaded any issues, they will need to be downloaded again in the new version before you can view them. (Please note, this process may take some time.) Going forward, all new issues will be available only in the updated version of this app.

If you are having problems verifying your subscription, please call 1-866-772-8791.

How do I access digital editions if I am a current print subscriber?

You will need to verify your subscription and sign in to see your available issues.
1
Tap the "Get Started" button below and follow the steps to verify your subscription.

2
After you verify your subscription and have your account information, tap the Library icon at the bottom of the screen and then tap "Sign In" in the top left corner.

3
Download your available issues and enjoy!

If you are having problems verifying your subscription, please call 1-866-772-8791.
